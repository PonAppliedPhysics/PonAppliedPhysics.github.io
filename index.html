ABOUT

I'm a Physics BS student at The Ohio State University with an Engineering Science minor and an applied physics / mechanical engineering focus. My coursework spans experimental physics, electronics, optics, quantum mechanics, machine learning, and mechanical engineering.

My work lives at the boundary between physical theory and practical engineering — whether that's building sensor validation pipelines for Honda's factory floor, machining AUV parts in the campus machine shop, or implementing neural networks that satisfy PDEs as physics constraints.

I'm particularly interested in mechanical engineering, robotics, data science, and ML/AI applied to real physical systems — problems where getting the physics right actually matters.
